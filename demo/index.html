FlipDiv

A space efficient 3D menu with CSS transitions.

Move your mouse towards the arrow — or swipe in from the arrow if you're on a touch device — to open. Test it with any page by appending a URL, like so:
?u=http://hakim.se.

FlipDiv can be positioned on any side of the screen:
top - right - bottom - left

Instructions and download at:
github.com/kireerik/FlipDiv

The name, FlipDiv, is not swedish, but Meny is. Meny is the original name of this cool menu concept. In the menu you can see Hakim El Hattab's great works and experiments. Feel free to check out them! Special thanks to Hakim El Hattab (@hakimel) and those who are contributed to the original project!
